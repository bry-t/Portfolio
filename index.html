About Me

Bryson Taeza

At 17, while still a senior in high school, I made the decision to join the United States Army. 2 months after I graduated I was getting off the bus at basic training.

I served in multiple capacities and units up until 2020. The last 8 years of my career I served in a leadership capacity.

While in a leadership role I was directly responsible for between 3 to 5 soldiers. I was able to develope a lot of self-discipline and leadership skills. Knowing that the lives of my men depended on me, my communication skills developed as well.

I left the Army as COVID was changing the world, and needed to find a way to use my skills in the new reality. I was able to enroll in Eleven Fifty Academy's Web Development course and have learned HTML, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and React.
